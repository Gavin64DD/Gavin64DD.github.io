History

The franchise originally started as a game series of the same name back in Septmeber 1986. However, the anime series is based off of Castlevania III (1989) and the most popular game in the series Symphony of the Night (1997). The last game in the series was in 2014 with Castlevania: Lords of Shadows 2. The anime started in July 2017 and gets a new season each year. There are currently 2 seasons with a 3rd one set to come out on Netflix later this year. This series started a renaissance of the series with two game collections and two of the main heros (Simon and Richter) being adding to the newest super smash bros roster
